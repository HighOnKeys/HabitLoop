## ðŸ’¡ The Solution

HabitLoop is an AI-driven system that adapts to your personal behavior patterns to help you cross the finish line.

### How It Works 
1.  **Set Your Goal:** Link your resource (YouTube playlist, documentation, or online course) and answer a few questions to establish your baseline.
2.  **Build Your Habit:** The system tracks your consistency, adapting to your rhythm by sending smart nudges and assigning short, manageable daily sessions.
3.  **Achieve & Grow:** By hitting small daily targets, you build a lasting routine and gain the confidence to tackle larger goals.

## ðŸ¤– The AI Engine

Our AI acts as a behavioral analyst rather than a generic chatbot. It follows a three-step loop: 

1.  **Observe (Input):** Tracks session duration, time of day patterns, missed days vs. completed days, and user difficulty feedback.
2.  **Decide (Process):** Calculates optimal nudge times, determines when to scale difficulty up or down, and identifies when social accountability is needed.
3.  **Act (Output):** Generates personalized schedules, context-sensitive reminders, and weekly growth insights.

*This approach ensures the AI is explainable, scalable, and practical.*
